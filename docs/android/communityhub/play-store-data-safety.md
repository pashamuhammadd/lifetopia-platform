# CommunityHub Play Store data-safety worksheet

Complete this against production behavior before submission; do not copy answers blindly into Play Console.

Known categories include account identity/email, user IDs, user-generated posts/comments, private messages, moderation reports, and an optional public Solana address. CommunityHub does not request or store seed phrases or private keys and does not provide trading, swap, staking, or custodial-wallet functions.

Confirm retention, deletion, encryption in transit, sharing, analytics/crash SDKs, precise data purposes, and whether every collected category is optional or required. The privacy policy must match the final answers exactly.

Account creation means Play submission must expose an in-app deletion path and an external account-deletion URL. This project does not yet claim that those deletion flows exist.
